Testing

Before high school, I had never worked with black and white or film photography. But after taking a couple of film photography classes in high school, I became more interested in both film and editing digital photos from color to black and white. The overall difference between black and white versus full color caught my interest because I kept wondering how my usual digital photography would feel if I made such an easy and quick edit.
