A Better Future

Over the course of this pandemic, we’ve heard questions of, “When will we return to normal?” or “What will normal be afterwards?” Who knows? It’s a crazy period of time that we’re living in with everything going on outside of our work lives and school lives. The stress of the world has mentally affected us and it seems as though there’s no end to it at all. It feels as though there’s something new to be added to this list of craziness we’ve experienced over the past year. As we take it all day by day, we can only hope that the next day is better than the previous one. No matter the time or place, we’re always hoping and looking towards this better future.
